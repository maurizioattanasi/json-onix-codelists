ONIX Code Lists Issue 47, October 2019
List 198: Product contact role
Value Description Notes
00 Metadata contact For queries and feedback concerning the metadata record itself
01 Accessibility request contact Eg for requests for supply of mutable digital files for conversion to other formats
02 Promotional contact Eg for requests relating to interviews, author events
03 Advertising contact Eg for co-op advertising
04 Review copy contact Eg for requests for review copies
05 Evaluation copy contact Eg for requests for approval or evaluation copies (particularly within education)
06 Permissions contact Eg for requests to reproduce or repurpose parts of the publication
07 Return authorisation contact Eg for use where authorisation must be gained from the publisher rather than the distributor or wholesaler
08 CIP / Legal deposit contact
